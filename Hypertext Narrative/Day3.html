Today we took a day trip to Kamakura by train. Kamakura is a beach town known for its Buddhist Temples.

To be honest, I was not able to properly enjoy the sights or take pictures because it was very hot and my feet were aching.

I spotted these cats while walking and they made me happy.

Eventually I found a cafe where I ate delicious curry with cheese and rice.

However I am lactose-intolerant and suffered even more for the rest of the day.

Kamakura was very beautiful and walking along the beach boardwalk is enjoyable. However the amount of tourists and visitors is overwhelming for such a small town. The trains are overcrowded and impossible to even fit on at times.
